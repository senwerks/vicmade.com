Introduction

BallaratMade is a multi-user blog created by the nerds behind Ballarat Hackerspace. In simple terms it's a git-backed markdown-based multi-user distributed-source blog engine (we'll write up a longer explanation soon).

Posts on this site are pulled from individual members personal Git repositories, allowing them to create posts about their projects while keeping control of their own content.

If you're a maker (of any kind) and would like to join this mad experiment in group-accountability and project-sharing, then follow the instructions here to submit your repo of markdown posts.
